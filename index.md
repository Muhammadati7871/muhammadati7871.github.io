## 🛡 Common Questions

**Does this software modify my server files?**
No, spixy only creates a bridge between your folder and the player. Your server files remain secure on your hosting provider.

**What happens if I make a mistake in the settings?**
You can use the "Reset" button in the settings menu to clear your current progress and start fresh.

**Do my players need to install spixy?**
No. Your players only download the `.exe` file that you build. They use that file to log into your server.

**How do I update mods later?**
Open your project in spixy, replace the old files with the new ones in the mods section, and click "Deploy Update." The launcher detects the change and prompts players to update the next time they open the game.
